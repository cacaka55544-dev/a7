Hardware Craftsmanship: 24K Gold Plating vs Zinc Alloys

By Luxury Hardware Metallurgist • 8 Min Read

Cheap fashion handbags cut costs by using cast zinc alloy buckles coated in thin gold-colored lacquer that chips away within months. **Solid Machined Brass Hardware** electroplated with a 3-micron layer of pure 24K gold creates permanent corrosion resistance and substantial weight. This guide explores hardware metallurgy.

1. CNC Machined Solid Brass Core

Every D-ring, turn-lock clasp, and strap buckle is CNC machined from solid brass blocks, eliminating brittle air bubbles found in zinc castings.

2. 3-Micron 24K Gold Electroplating

Multi-stage electroplating binds pure gold to brass at a molecular level, preventing tarnishing even in coastal saltwater environments.
