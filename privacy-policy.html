Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 23, 2026

At Majestic Gallery Bag, accessible from majesticgallerybag.com, protecting visitor privacy and leathercraft collector data is a core operational commitment. This Privacy Policy details the categories of information collected and recorded by our platform and how we utilize it.

For any inquiries regarding this policy or data protection, please contact our team at privacy@majesticgallerybag.com.

1. Information Categories Collected

We process personal and non-personal data for legitimate operational needs:

  • Personal Data: Collected when you purchase luxury leather goods, request custom monogramming, or subscribe to our Venetian Leather Registry newsletter. This includes your name, shipping address, email address, contact phone number, and payment credentials.
  • Monogramming Details: Custom initials and leather finish choices submitted for bespoke saddle-stitched items.
  • Cookie Data: Sourced via cookies to remember user preferences, maintain active sessions, and deliver optimized 3D gallery performance.

2. Purpose of Data Processing

Majestic Gallery Bag processes collected data to:

  • Fulfill, ship, and insure luxury leather handbag orders and custom monogrammed items.
  • Communicate order status, tracking information, and concierge support.
  • Send exclusive invitations to private handbag drops and atelier events.
  • Serve relevant, non-intrusive ads through platforms like Google Ads.
  • Detect, prevent, and address system vulnerabilities, unauthorized access, or fraudulent transactions.

3. Log File Analysis

Majestic Gallery Bag follows standard protocols by maintaining log files. These files document visitor traffic when accessing the site. This is a default practice of hosting servers and forms part of hosting analytics. Information includes IP addresses, browser client, ISP details, date stamps, and referring/exit coordinates. This data is fully aggregated and is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

4. Third-Party Advertising & Tracking Policies

We work with trusted marketing partners, including Google Ads, who serve cookies and web beacons on our site. These third-party tracking services collect aggregated data to analyze marketing performance and serve personalized advertising based on your previous browsing history.

5. User Rights (GDPR & CCPA Compliance)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you possess specific data protection rights, including: the right to access stored data, the right to edit inaccuracies, the right to request deletion ("right to be forgotten"), the right to object to data processing, and the right to port your data. To exercise these rights, please email us directly.
